What is CNC Wood Cutting?

CNC (Computer Numerical Control) uses computerized routers to cut, carve, and shape wood from digital design files. Unlike manual methods, CNC ensures millimeter-accurate cuts, consistent repeatability, and the ability to produce intricate shapes and engravings with minimal waste. At Mahak Enterprise we operate modern CNC routers run by experienced operators who translate CAD/CAM files into flawless wooden components.

Our Process

  1. Design submission: Upload DXF, DWG, PDF or CAD files — we can also create files if needed.

  3. CNC programming: Accurate toolpath generation and test simulation to avoid errors.
  4. Cutting & finishing: Precision routing followed by any sanding, chamfering, or pre-finishing required.
